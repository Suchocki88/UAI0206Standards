Understanding AI: A Conceptual Approach
Chapter 2, Lesson 2.6: Building the Core of Your Project (Project Day)

Lesson 2.6 is a Project Day where students apply their learning about AI capabilities (from Lessons 2.3-2.5) to develop foundational elements of creative projects through AI collaboration. This lesson integrates writing standards, technology literacy, creative development, and critical reflection on human-AI partnership.

Crosscutting Concepts

Patterns
Different patterns may be observed at each of the scales at which a system is studied.
Lesson 2.6 Alignment: Students observe patterns in AI responses‚Äîtendency toward common archetypes, generic suggestions when prompts are vague, improved specificity with structured requests.
Cause and Effect
Cause and effect relationships may be used to predict phenomena in natural or designed systems.
Lesson 2.6 Alignment: Students identify cause-effect relationships: structured prompts cause better responses; vague requests cause generic outputs; specific creative direction causes AI to follow rather than lead.
